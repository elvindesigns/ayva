FAQs

Answers to your questions

Do you charge for counselling?

Our counselling session is free for students seeking study opportunities in the United Kingdom. Charges may apply for applications to other destinations.

Does free processing cover application fees?

No, university application fees must be paid directly to the institution.

Do you guarantee visas?

No agency can guarantee visas, but we guide you through the process to maximise your chances.

Which countries do you handle student applications for?

The United Kingdom, Canada, the United States of America, Ireland, Germany, Australia, Malta, Dubai, and more.

What makes Ayva Consults different?

We combine student recruitment, visa support, and travel services all in one place, giving clients a complete solution.
